Think-Do Tank Group Press Statement

April 9, 2020

A press statement issued by the Think-Do Tank Group of the Civil Society Coalition on Sustainable Development (CSCSD) on emerging developments in fighting the COVID-19 pandemic in Nigeria. The Think-Do Tank Group is the national arm of Civil Society Coalition on Sustainable Development(CSCSD), which is a coalition of over 2,000-member networks, NGOs, media, professional associations, including academia, youth and women groups collaborating with the government to accelerate the implementation of SGDs in Nigeria.

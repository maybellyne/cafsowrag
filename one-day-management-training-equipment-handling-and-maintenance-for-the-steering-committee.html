One-Day Management Training, Equipment Handling and Maintenance for the Steering Committee

Maybellyne January 30, 2012

Women’s Economic Empowerment through Local Technology in Rural Communities of Akinyele Local Government Area of Oyo State, Nigeria is a project supported by the Social Development Fund of the French Embassy in Nigeria. The project is located in Alagbede village in Akinyele Local Government Area of Oyo State, Nigeria. Alagbede is equidistant to the major marketing communities along the Ojoo/Igbo-Oloyin and Alade/Iware axis of Akinyele local government area.

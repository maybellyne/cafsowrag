Letter in Support of CAFSO-initiated Bill on GBV

Maybellyne August 27, 2014

Gender-based violence is topical and rampant in Oyo State, and seeking justice to address the issue has become a huge problem. We have organized along with key stakeholders a technical workshop on curbing gender-based violence through pushing for the establishment of the Gender Equality Unit so as to facilitate access to gender justice at the High Court of Justice in Oyo State.

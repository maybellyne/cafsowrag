Attend a Two-Day Workshop on Proposal Writing

February 5, 2018

CAFSO-WRAG FOR DEVELOPMENT (with support from Maria Ebun Foundation) presents a Two-Day Training Workshop on Fundraising and Proposal Writing PRESENTS A TWO-DAY TRAINING with a special session on NGO Management and Organizational Profiling
TARGET: Specially designed for CSOs, NGOs, CBOs, not-for-profit, small scale social entrepreneur, startups etc.
DATE: Wednesday 14 and Thursday 15 February 2018
TIME: 10.. 00 a.m daily
VENUE: University of Ibadan Conference Centre and Hotel, Abadina, Ibadan

Fundraising is the biggest milestone for non-profits and yet a big challenge because of the shrinking space for CSOs. There is donor fatigue as well as a shift in focus and geographic coverage. Even when there are opportunities for funding, some development actors lack the skills to write fundable proposals while some actually deviate from donors’ guidelines. In light of this, this workshop is designed to provide useful tools and skills for fundraising, proposal writing and diversification from grant sourcing to profit-making through social business. As participants, you will learn how to:

  1. Profile your organization for securing grants
  2. Develop suitable strategies for fundraising
  3. Practice proposal writing types/methods/parts
  4. Formulate a log frame fashioned after UNICEF, UNDP, USAID, or EU styles
  5. Develop monitoring and evaluation techniques etc

Additional benefits include: providing the addresses/links of donors funding development work; presenting alternative sources of income for NGOs; free 260+ page book on post-2015 Development Agenda; a booklet on SDGs; handouts and certificate of participation.

FACULTY

  • Development experts of over 20 years’ experience
  • Specialists from universities on monitoring and evaluation
  • Astute gurus in entrepreneurship
  • Experts that have won grants from EU, UNDP, SDF, USAID, OSIWA etc

Fee: N10,000 covering registration, course materials, lunch and refreshment. This is a subsidized rate; it used to be N25,000.

Payment should be made to: CAFSO-WRAG for Development. Guaranty Trust Bank a/c No: 0029537931. Since the date is fast approaching, it is advisable payment is made immediately for logistic reasons rather than paying on the day of the workshop. DON’T PAY ON THE DAY OF THE WORKSHOP
LODGING: In the interest of out-of-Ibadan participants, accommodation at a reduced rate for participants is available at UI Hotel and Conference Centre. After payment, send your details to info@cafsowrag4development.org and cafsowinco58@yahoo.co.uk for documentation.
